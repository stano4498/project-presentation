Why This Project?

I chose to create AgriLink because many small-scale farmers in Kenya struggle with outdated methods, lack of expert access, unpredictable weather, and limited market opportunities.

What Problem Does It Solve?

  • Lack of modern farming knowledge
  • Difficulty in accessing smart tools or platforms
  • Poor yields due to outdated practices
  • Trouble accessing wider markets
